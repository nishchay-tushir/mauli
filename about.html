HEALTH, SAFETY & ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY

──────────────────

At Mauli Electrical, we firmly believe that our long-term business success is contingent upon our capacity to consistently enhance service quality while upholding the well-being of people and the environment. Consequently, we are dedicated to fulfilling our customers' expectations and perpetually endeavor to augment the efficiency of our Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment (QHSE) Management System. To attain this objective, our unwavering focus will be on the following...

  • Provision of safe working environment, prevention of injury, ill health and planning for enhanced emergency preparedness.

  • Minimizing adverse impact of our activities on the environment to prevent the pollution and to preserve natural resources.

  • Optimal utilization of men, machine, finance and other resources.

  • Devising planning systems for effective implementation.

  • Strong organizational support through human resources development.

  • Development of reliable vendors for a higher degree of Quality Assurance.

  • Complying with contractual requirements.

  • ommunicating openly with stakeholders and making them aware on the QHSE aspects.

  • Compliance of applicable legal and other requirements.
